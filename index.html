ABOUT US

Eden Concrete is a full-service family run building, construction and renovations business based in Johannesburg, South Africa. Founded in 1992, we have been serving Gauteng as one of the more respected building companies for over 25 years.
HISTORY
Originally the company was a part of Eden Concrete who’s specialty was laying concrete in the residential sector. The company was founded on the principles of honest work and quality workmanship. We have specialised in home improvements and custom additions for over two decades. With our growing reputation for quality, attention to detail and the upmost professionalism, we have expanded into the turnkey building industry. Today we build the finest homes and structures within our client’s budgets and time frame without sacrificing quality and workmanship. We stay current with the latest building technologies and regulations to ensure your home gets the most up to date construction methods available.
A FAMILY BUSINESS
A trade passed down for generations, an insatiable desire to see our clients satisfied, work completed on time and with cleanliness, competitive prices thanks to discounts from our suppliers; these are all the right ingredients that will help you realise your home improvement project’s completion.
PASSIONATE EXPERTSA
Andrew is invested heavily in the growth and development of his team and under his supervision has put together a highly capable and competent team to take on the demands of any project. We understand the importance of innovation and professionalism and work with the best people to achieve this. Our team consists of master builders and skilled laborers. We are able to offer integrated services for you residential and commercial projects and we manage the construction, from plan to completion. We’re an ever-expanding family of design and construction craftsmen and project management experts who believe in delivering high quality solutions. Together our team has worked on a number of challenging projects ranging from uplifting homes, extension projects, renovations, to new home construction. We have experience managing complex construction projects and a proven ability to brainstorm any problem. We love a challenge.
